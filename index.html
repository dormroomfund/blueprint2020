FAQ

Investor Track FAQ Founder Track FAQ

1. When should I apply?
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis until May 15th. We encourage you to apply as soon as possible!

2. Who are you looking for?
You’re an underrepresented minority student who identifies as Black, Latinx or Indigenous. We welcome students of all backgrounds to apply. You could be a freshman majoring in Poli-sci or a PhD student studying Machine Learning. As long as you’re a full-time student graduating 2022 or later, you’re invited to apply to join the Dorm Room Fund team. If this is the summer before you start a full-time program we encourage you to apply (ex: B-School, Law School, Incoming freshman). You have passion for entrepreneurship and curiosity about new innovations, businesses, or technology. Don’t worry if you don’t have a ton of experience yet. We value life-long learners and folks driven by curiosity. You have hustle and like to tackle challenges head on. Operating in the world of VC takes a lot of work, so this is something you should be prepared to do.

3. What is the selection process?
After reviewing your application, a member of our team may reach out to you for a 30 minute video interview. This interview is to get to know you better and learn more about what excites you. We will notify all applicants of their status by May 31st.

4. What is the time commitment?
From June 27 to August 8th, participants will meet for two hours, once a week, for 6 weeks on (day of the week). The application lists the dates of the sessions and to successfully complete the program we’ll need you to commit to all sessions.

5. Where will sessions be held?
Our sessions will be 100% remote. This gives you the flexibility to be a part of our community regardless of where you’ll be spending your summer.

6. What does a typical Blueprint session look like?
The Dorm Room Fund Team is partnering with some of the best VCs, Angel Investors and Founders in the game to teach you a new topic each week. Sessions will be a combination lecture style teaching, speaker series and small group projects.

7. Do I have to pay for this program?
No. At Dorm Room Fund, we aim to make entrepreneurship and education as accessible as possible. While the program is free for you, we do invest time, energy and resources into making our program great. The only thing that we ask for is that you commit to giving the program 100%.

8. What is Dorm Room Fund?
We're a student-run venture fund backed by First Round Capital. We invest in startups where at least one person on the founding team is a student (undergraduate or graduate). Our investment team is run by student leaders, entrepreneurs, and innovators across the country.

9. Who should I contact if I have more questions?
JeanClaude Dorsainvil at JeanClaude@dormroomfund.com Reach out with any questions or concerns!
